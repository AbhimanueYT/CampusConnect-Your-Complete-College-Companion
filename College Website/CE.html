DIPLOMA IN CIVIL ENGINEERING


Civil Engineering includes the planning, design, construction, maintenance and operation of the infrastructure that surrounds us. The Department of Civil Engineering of SCMS college of Polytechnics, Vaikkara provide students with a sound fundamental knowledge to design solutions for complex civil engineering problems. The department provides a right kind of environment for the students to groom themselves for innovative and challenging near future.

Survey camps and practical training are part of curriculum aims at familiarizing the students with real time problems. Students gain practical experience from field visits to industries, construction sites, dams and irrigation structures. The students are exposed to the main areas of Structural Engineering, Transportation Engineering, Irrigation Engineering, Environmental Engineering, and Construction Technology & Managament.


Civil Engineering Students Association (CESA)


The association exhibits Civil Construction models and conducts workshop to get practical knowledge about the latest techniques used in Civil Engineering field.
