DIPLOMA IN COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING


The mission of computer engineering department of SCP is nurturing globally competent Computer Engineers capable of taking challenges in the industry and Research & Development activities. The course emphasizes on the basics of Software development, hardware, networking and testing.

Close interaction with the industry has led to the evolution of a teaching system, which produces diligent and erudite engineers. The department has highly qualified & dynamic staff to motivate the students through projects & competitions.

Since its inception, the department has been producing computer engineers of high-caliber, who make the workforce in numerous companies through campus placement.


Skilled Programmer's Association in Computer Engineering (SP@CE)


The association conducts hands on training in IoT, JAVA and many languages / language tools to improve the practical skill of the students.
